**Badge** — compact status / label pill. Tone carries meaning (evidence, compliance, risk, validated), not decoration.

```jsx
<Badge tone="safe" variant="soft" dot>Validated</Badge>
<Badge tone="compliance">Citation required</Badge>
<Badge tone="evidence" size="sm">12 citations</Badge>
```

Tones: `neutral | accent | evidence | compliance | risk | safe`. Variants: `soft | solid | outline`. `dot` adds a leading status dot.
